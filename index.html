    Sculpture, an artistic form in which hard or plastic materials are worked into three-dimensional art objects.
    The designs may be embodied in freestanding objects, in reliefs on surfaces, or in environments
    ranging from tableaux to contexts that envelop the spectator.

  • About us

    National Gallery of Modern Art helps people to look at the works of modern art with greater joy, understanding and knowledge by extending their relationship with our daily life and experiencing them as vital expressions of the human spirit.

    Principle

    To ensure quality and to set and maintain standards of excellence.

    Objectives

    To acquire and preserve works of modern art from 1850s onward To organize, maintain and develop galleries for permanent display To organize special exhibitions not only in its own premises but in other parts of the country and abroad.
